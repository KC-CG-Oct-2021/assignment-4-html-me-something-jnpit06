Joy and Happiness

Joy, what is it? According to the Oxford Languages: Joy is a feeling of great pleasure and happiness. Joy is an Middle English word that orginated in the 1800's as a Latin word, Gaudere which means rejoice.

Happiness, what is it? According to the Oxford Languages: Happiness is the state of being happy. Happiness is an Old English word from the 1500's that means "good luck", "success", and "contentment".

Is there really a difference between Joy and Happiness?

One of the great questions of life: Is there really a difference between Joy and Happiness? If you walk down the street stop and ask complete strangers the question, "Is there a really a difference between Joy and Happiness?" You would get many different answers. According to most psychologist: Joy is more consistent and is cultivated internally when one finds peace with who you are, your purpose in life, and the season that you are currently in. Happiness happens more from external factors: people, places, things, thoughts and events.
